        Dropout indicator & $1^{D}_{i,j}$ & Whether gene $i$ in cell $j$ has been kept & $\sim Ber(\pi^{D}_{i,j})$\\
        Count & $Y_{i,j}$ & Final simulated count for gene $i$ in cell $j$ after dropout & $1^{D}_{i,j}Y^{'}_{i,j}$\\
